Brief

Our service offers automation of billing processes in SAP and subsequent delivery to customers. The service streamlines the billing process, reducing manual efforts and errors. The automated invoices are generated in SAP and delivered to customers promptly, ensuring timely payment and improved customer experience.


Process Description


1) Delivery creation:

This step involves creating deliveries in SAP, which include all relevant information such as product details, quantity, and delivery date. The deliveries are created based on customer orders and are used to keep track of the products being sent to customers.


2) Shipment creation:

After creating deliveries, shipments are created to provide an accurate record of the products being shipped to customers. This step includes information such as shipping address, transportation details, and expected delivery date. The shipment creation process is linked to the deliveries, ensuring that the correct products are shipped to the correct customers.


3) Invoicing:

In this step, the billing process is automated in SAP, generating invoices based on the deliveries and shipments information. The invoices include all relevant information such as product details, quantity, and total amount due. The automated invoicing process ensures that all necessary information is included and reduces the risk of manual errors.


4) Email delivery to customers:

The final step is to send the generated invoices to customers via email. This step provides customers with a convenient and efficient way to receive their bills and makes it easier for them to make timely payments. The email delivery process is automated, ensuring that invoices are delivered promptly and efficiently to customers.


Maintenance:

We also provide ongoing maintenance and support to ensure that our clients can use the service without interruption. We constantly monitor the system, and make updates as necessary, to guarantee that it runs smoothly and provides accurate results.
