Business Intelligence

Business Intelligence (BI) services and dashboards are tools that help organizations make data-driven decisions by providing insights and analysis of business data. BI services typically include a range of technologies, such as data mining, data visualization, and reporting, that enable businesses to transform raw data into actionable information. Dashboards are interactive visual displays that provide a summary of key performance indicators (KPIs) and other relevant data, allowing users to quickly and easily track the health of their business. By using BI services and dashboards, businesses can identify trends, uncover opportunities, and track progress towards their goals.
